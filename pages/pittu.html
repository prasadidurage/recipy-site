Ingredients:

For the Pittu:

  • 2 cups roasted rice flour
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
In a large bowl, mix the roasted rice flour with salt. Gradually add water while mixing with your fingers to create a crumbly, grainy texture. Take a Pittu steamer and layer it with grated coconut at the bottom, followed by a layer of the rice flour mixture. Continue layering, finishing with coconut on top.
